About Me

Musician

Since graduating from DePaul University in 2015, Zach has worked as a professional freelance musican for bands and theare productions in and around the Austin area. Capyac, Dale Watson, The Watters, ZACH Theatre, Zilker Hillside Theatre, are just a few of the groups he has performed with.

Acting & Modeling

Zach has been a part-time professional model & actor since early 2017. He has had roles and apperances in advertisments for companies such as: Amazon, Apple, Dell, LegalZoom, ConocoPhilips, Ascension, and HEB among others. He had a brief reaccuring role as a "Hero" zombie in "Fear The Walking Dead".

Co-Author

In 2018 Zach had the opportunity to work with world renown musician, and drummer Nate Smith. Zach transcribed over 30 minutes of drumming to create one of the most incredible collection of drum transcriptions available. It has sold all over the world in hard back, ebook, and pdf.

Teaching Artist

Zach began teaching learners of all ages as soon as he returned from Austin. He currently teaches piano lessons to students ages 6-12 through Westlake Lessons, and has worked for multiple non-profits teaching percussion and drum lessons in underserved communities throughout the Austin area.
